Vaccinate Karnataka

This Campaign was launched by the Krs Party to pressurise the Government to increase the vaccination pace in the State. As part of the campaign, All Elected Representatives of the Congress Party pledged 1 Crore Rupees from their MP/MLA LAD Funds which raised 100 Crores to start an independent Vaccination Drive by tying-up with Pvt Hospitals to provide vaccination to the people of the state for free. KRS President Krishna Reddy & along with all the Office Bearers of KRS led this campaign in a spirited manner which sent shivers across the Leadership that eventually had to reject the proposal of Krs Party to save its face. Then, the campaign was modified to initiate awareness among the people by involving the kids through a unique programme. As part of the campaign, hundreds of children and students in the state shared videos on Vaccine Awareness on Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram through #VaccinateKarnataka hashtag.

Awareness Campgain

The Karnataka Congress party has been continuously fighting against the failure of the Central & State Governments led by the BJP, their anti-people policies and price hike and has launched 'Janadhwani Yatra' with the motive to reach out & consolidate the anger among the people against the BJP Government. This yatra was launched on March 3rd at Devanahalli in Bengaluru rural district and the Janadhwani Yatra conferences held at Devanahalli and Chikkaballapur were got great success. In the coming days, The Janadhwani Yatra is scheduled to be held across the state at all district & taluk centres. In this consolidated effort to bring Congress Party back to power in the upcoming state assembly elections, Congress leaders in the state will tour 100 constituencies in the state in the next one year through this 'Janadhwani Yatra'.

Sonkita Sarkara

This inhuman government also made money on people's dead bodies by amassing money in buying beds, Remidesivir, ventilators, gloves, PPE kits and sanitiser.
